Financial accounting

teachers name- eknath teli , arjuna jadhav

Financial accounting is a specific branch of accounting involving a process of recording, summarizing,
and reporting the myriad of transactions resulting from business operations over a period of time.
In simple terms, financial accounting is the practice of accounting for all money going in and out of an organization.
It involves recording, classifying, summarizing, and analyzing all financial transactions. Recording Transactions are recorded as either a debit or a credit.
